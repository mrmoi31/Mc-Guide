Buried Treasure Diamonds and Clay

Buried Treasure spawns in beach biomes randomly and are easiest to find using a buried treasure map which can be obtained from a shipwreck or ocean ruins. A useful thing to know is that buried treasure always spawns at chunk coordinates 9 ~ 9.

Diamonds and clay linked generation? Find a clay patch that is "circular," and find the center Move a specific amount of blocks based on the biome Dig straight down **for the counting:** if you hit the edge of a chunk while counting, go to the other side of the chunk and continue in the same direction(towards your start) 2 blocks south in regular lake biomes (20 - 30 tests) 2 blocks north in river biomes (5 - 7 tests) 8 blocks north in swamp biomes( 10 - 11 tests)
